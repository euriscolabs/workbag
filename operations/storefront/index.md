# Storefront

Everything related to selling products and presenting Eurisco Labs online.

## Properties

| Property | Purpose | Status |
|----------|---------|--------|
| [Website](website/) | Landing page, project showcase, blog | Active |
| [Webshop](webshop/) | Product sales (Shopify) | Planned |

## Sales Model
- Own webshop (primary channel)
- Print-on-demand / made-to-order
- Kit sales: curated hard-to-source parts for people who want to build it themselves
- No inventory risk where possible
