## Content Strategy
Document the journey. Every project is a video: research, design, prototype, test, iterate.

My questions during research guide the flow of the video. There's a problem statement at the beginning, and the video is a visual representation of the thought process that leads to interactions with the material world through the product.

## Keywords / Tone
- Agency, direct action
- "Let's build our own"
- Detours, tangents, going off on tangents
- Hands-on labs

## Content Pipeline
1. **Idea** — Add to backlog
2. **Research** — Gather data, form hypothesis
3. **Script/Outline** — Structure the narrative
4. **Film** — B-roll, process shots, talking head
5. **Edit** — Cut, add graphics, sound
6. **Thumbnail** — Design eye-catching thumbnail
7. **Publish** — Title, description, tags, end screens
8. **Repurpose** — Extract clips for Shorts/TikTok/Reels
