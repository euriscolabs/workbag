## Principle
**Knowledge is open, polish is paid.** Anyone can reproduce the work from the open research. Patrons pay for the shortcut.

## Subscription Tiers

| Tier | Price | What's Included |
|------|-------|-----------------|
| Free (public) | $0 | Research papers, raw data, blog posts, basic STLs |
| Supporter | $5/mo | Print-ready files, assembly guides, BOM with supplier links, build walkthroughs, early access |
| Pro | $15/mo | Parametric CAD (STEP/F3D), slicer profiles, direct Q&A |
| Sponsor | $50/mo | Vote on next projects, name in credits/papers |
