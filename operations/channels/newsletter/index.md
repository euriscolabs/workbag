## Strategy
- **Cadence**: Bi-weekly (adjust based on content volume)

- **Tone**: Personal, behind-the-scenes, what I learned this week

## Content Structure
1. **What I'm working on** — Current project update
2. **What I learned** — Interesting finding, technique, or rabbit hole
3. **What's coming** — Upcoming video, product, or research piece
4. **Links** — Relevant resources, tools, articles I found useful

| Paid subscribers | Patreon Supporter+ | Exclusive deep-dives, files |

## Signup Flow
1. Visitor lands on website or YouTube
2. CTA leads to signup form
3. Welcome email with intro + links to best content
4. Regular cadence begins
