# Roadmap

## Video Production Roadmap

### Phase 1 — Establish the channel (months 1–3)

| # | Video | Why first |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Tool organization / inlay system** | Workshop setup content, popular genre, builds filming space. Opens with a personal context beat — who I am and what this is — so no separate intro video is needed. |
| 2 | **Workbench / workspace setup** | Completes the studio, gives a clean place to film everything else |

### Phase 2 — First product arc (months 3–5)

| # | Video | Why here |
|---|---|---|
| 3 | **Lamp: research + design** | Storyboard exists, science is done — film the design/prototype phase now |
| 4 | **Lamp: building the polishing/finishing setup** | The manufacturing infrastructure is itself a video |
| 5 | **Lamp: prototype → product, shop launch** | Payoff video, first product goes live in the webshop |

### Phase 3 — Expand (months 6–12)

Repeat the arc for the next project. By now: filming setup ready, working shop, manufacturing infrastructure, audience following along.

> The lamp is split into two parts intentionally — "I designed this, now I need to build the tools to make it properly" is a natural cliffhanger that brings people back.

> No standalone channel intro video. The first 60–90s of video 01 carries that context, and a channel trailer can be cut from that footage for the YouTube channel page.
