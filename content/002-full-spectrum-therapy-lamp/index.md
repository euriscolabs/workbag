## Summary

Part 1 of the full-spectrum therapy lamp arc. Covers the science of winter sickness, vitamin D, light therapy, and the design of a safe, evidence-based lamp. Ends with a working prototype — manufacturing comes in video 008.

## Key Messages

- Why winter makes us feel bad (vitamin D, circadian rhythm, light exposure)
- What full-spectrum light actually is
- How to build one safely (LEDs, drivers, thermal management)
- First prototype reveal

## Goal

Show the research, design, and build process for a DIY full-spectrum therapy lamp — covering the science of why light matters for health, why commercial options fall short, and how to build your own.

## Outline

1. Hook: overstimulating stress montage → lamp click → everything better
2. Cold open: hiking, cabin, fireplace — cozy contrast
3. Intro: winter reality vs. cozy fantasy
4. Science: why we get sick in winter, vitamin D, how sunlight works, tanning beds, full-spectrum light, UVB lamps, safety
5. Why build my own: commercial lamps don't cut it
6. Design: sketches + CAD
7. Parts list: LEDs, electronics, housing, mechanical
8. The build: 3D modeling, printing, soldering, assembly
9. Testing & results: lux measurements, personal experience
10. Outro & CTA

## Approach

- Format: long-form main video + short clips
- Style: build log + science explainer
- Assets needed: build footage, diagrams, spectrum charts, science graphics
