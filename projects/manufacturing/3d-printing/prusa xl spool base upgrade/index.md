## Overview

A drybox-based spool holder upgrade for the Prusa XL, keeping filament dry and accessible during multi-material prints.

## Problem

Stock spool holders on the Prusa XL expose filament to ambient humidity, which degrades hygroscopic materials like PETG, Nylon, and TPU over time.

## Approach

Use the community Sicco Box design as a starting point for a drybox spool holder system compatible with the Prusa XL series.

## Open Questions

- Does the Sicco Box design need modifications for the 5-tool setup?
- Desiccant strategy — silica gel pouches or rechargeable dehumidifier?
- PTFE tube routing from drybox to extruder
