Advanced 3D Printing Method

Goal: Increase Mechanical strength of printed parts

Method: Use two phase per layer. First phase normal 3d printing, prints on layer, second phase using a precise laser reheat the entire layer at once to "remelt" the plastic increasing the bonding between layer lines and between the two layer (current and layer bellow).
